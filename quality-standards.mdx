## Arabica Specialty Grade (80+ Cup Score)

- **Cup score:** 80+
- **Screen size:** 17+
- **Defects:** Max 5 defects
- **Flavor:** Clean, balanced, sweet
- **Processing:** Mostly washed / honey

## What Buyers Look For

1. Consistency
2. Low defects
3. Good aroma
4. No moisture issues
5. Transparent sourcing
