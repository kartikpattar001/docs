title: " # Sourcing & Farmer Relations"
---


The best Arabica beans come from:

- Sakleshpur
- Chikmagalur
- Coorg

## Questions to Ask Farmers

- Moisture content
- Processing type (washed/natural)
- Screen size
- Cup score
- Harvest date
- Defect count
- Available quantity

\> ⚠️ Never buy without checking moisture content first.
