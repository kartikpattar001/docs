## Mandatory Documents

1. IEC (Importer Exporter Code)  

2. GST Registration  

3. FSSAI License  

4. Phytosanitary Certificate  

5. Certificate of Origin  

6. Commercial Invoice  

7. Packing List  

8. Bill of Lading / Airway Bill  

## Optional Documents (Adds Value)

- Organic certificate  

- Rainforest Alliance certificate  

- Fairtrade certificate
