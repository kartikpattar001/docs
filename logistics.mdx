## Packaging

- 60 kg jute bags  

- Vacuum bags for premium quality  

## Container Types

- 20 ft → 19-20 tons coffee  

- 40 ft → 26-27 tons coffee  

## Shipping Methods

- **FOB:** Buyer pays after loading  

- **CIF:** You handle insurance  

- **EXW:** Buyer picks from warehouse  

\> ⚠️ Always get pre-shipment sample approval from buyers.
